Chinatown Never Sleeps

A personal essay about burnout, resilience, and the city that never stops moving.

The city of New York never really stops moving. There's always something going on in the streets, whether it's someone rushing home after a late shift, a truck unloading boxes before morning, or a subway roaring below. That constant motion flows through Chinatown. From sunrise to dusk, the place is full of scents and sounds. At the core of all that movement, however, is something heavier, with Chinatown illustrating the hard work and fatigue that comes from the city that never sleeps.
